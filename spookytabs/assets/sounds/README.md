## Sound Requirements

- Format: MP3 (for compatibility)
- Ambient loops: 30-60 seconds, seamless loop
- Effects: 1-3 seconds, quick and subtle
- Volume: Normalized, not too loud
- Quality: 128-192 kbps (balance size/quality)

## Sources for Royalty-Free Sounds

- Freesound.org
- Zapsplat.com
- Pixabay (audio section)
- Create your own with Audacity

## Tips

- Keep ambient sounds subtle and non-intrusive
- Effects should be quick and not jarring
- Test at different volumes
- Ensure seamless looping for ambient tracks
- Consider adding slight reverb for atmosphere
